CMAKE_INTERPROCEDURAL_OPTIMIZATION_<CONFIG>
-------------------------------------------

.. versionadded:: 3.9

目标的\ :prop_tgt:`INTERPROCEDURAL_OPTIMIZATION_<CONFIG>`\ 默认值。

information.
